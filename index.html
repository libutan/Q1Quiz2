Bambi

Bambi, who is a young deer has new companions: Thumper, the rabbit, and Flower, the skunk. As a young deer, his mother, and father, The Great Prince, gives him lessons that there are always dangers lurking in the forest, and how to avoid hunters. He even met Faline, a doe whose age is as similar as his. A tragedy occured when his mother was killed in an attempt to escape the hunters. He wandered alone in the forest, thinking he has sepereated ways, until he met Thumper, and Flower again. He asks is father, what happened, until he was told the truth. He would learn to become independent, and would be triumph with Faline.
